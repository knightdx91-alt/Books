## Author planning notes (from a loose "Demon Academy" premise page — NOT prose; author's own outline)

MC Zack is taken into hell where he is told he is a greater demon. He did not know he was a demon, greater or otherwise, so this is a surprise. His father was the Demon Lord General Bal. His mother knew that Bal was a demon, and that there was a chance zack would be a demon as well. The only problem is: His mother was part angel. So zack is now part-demon and part-angel. In his mother it wasn't noticeable but zack's demon side amplified it. No one knows this at first, until the testing when it's determined that he has proficiency in not only demonic magics but angelic as well. This causes problems.

The Demon Lord General Bal governs the third ring of Hell. Zack's mother met Bal in his human form while he was exploring earth. Once Bal found out that Zack's mother was pregnant, Bal knew he'd have to leave as being around a Demon Lords infernal energy would make zack's demonic side mature faster, so to make sure zack could have a normal life, Bal returned to Hell and left zack and Carina on earth.

Being the son of a Demon Lord and an ex-arch angel, Zack possesses the ability to survive on both the angelic and infernal planes. His angelic side doesn't really come into play until the second book.
