## Macro-Structure

**Chosen structure: FIVE MOVEMENTS with a SLOW-BURN-TO-ERUPTION dynamic — chapters
that SWELL as she sinks into the past and then VIOLENTLY SHORTEN as the ash falls.**
Sectioned as **Movements** (dated, place-named), not "ACT ONE/TWO/THREE."

**Why this premise demands it (not a clean 3-act at 25/50/75):**
1. The story is a *nine-year immersion* bracketed by a two-day modern frame at the front
   and a two-day catastrophe at the back. A symmetrical three-act staircase would flatten
   exactly the thing that makes it Outlander-scale: the long, uneven, sink-into-a-world
   middle. The premise wants **lumpy, asymmetric movements**, not equal acts.
3. The eruption is a *compressed detonation* after an 85% pressure-build. That is the
   textbook case for **slow-burn + short explosive final movement**: the final movement's
   chapters get shorter and shorter (a family talking about bread runs 3,400w; the door
   closing runs 1,600w) so the reading *speed* accelerates into the surge even as the story
   time collapses to seconds. Uniform 5,400w chapters cannot do that.
4. **Chapter length is a structural signal here, on purpose:** the modern frame is brisk
   (short/medium chapters, one 1,800w crossing-punch); Greece and Campania *swell* into long
   immersive chapters (up to 12,000w — the winter, the wedding, the Pompeii day, the Fenn
   interview) because the book's argument is that the past is where she finally *lives*; then
   the ash movement guts the chapter lengths to render panic and loss. The shape enacts the
   theme.

**Jittered turning-point map (deliberately OFF the quarter-marks; back-loaded for slow-burn):**

|---|---|---|
| Crossing over (modern → 70 AD) | Ch.4 (crossing) → Ch.5 (landing) | ~6–10% |

Note the asymmetry: the "midpoint" lands at ~55%, and the third turn is jammed to ~86% —
this is a slow-burn whose catastrophe occupies only the final ~15%, which is the point.

**Section labels (Movements, dated/place-named — reader-visible or internal, author's call):**
- **Movement I — The Drawer and the Door** (England/the coast, late 1990s → the crossing) · Ch.1–5
- **Movement II — Learning to Exist** (Greece, 70 AD) · Ch.6–13
- **Movement III — The Life She Built** (Campania, ~72–78 AD) · Ch.14–18
- **Movement IV — The Arithmetic** (Campania, the WHEN and the rising dread) · Ch.19–27
- **Movement V — Ash** (the Bay of Naples, late summer 79 AD) · Ch.28–36

**Chapter-count logic:** 36 chapters (down from 45). The count FELL OUT OF THE BEATS, not
from `word_target ÷ floor`: adjacent modern-frame and dread beats were fused into single
richer chapters (Coordinates+Survey; No-Name+the-Mules-refusal; Leaving-Greece+Marriage;
Telling+Preparing; the door-calls+the-law-holds), while the catastrophe was SPLINTERED into
short punch chapters. Length follows the beat, so the granularity is coarse in the immersive
movements and fine in the ash.

**Length range:** **1,600w (Ch.36, the door closing) to 12,000w (Ch.11, the Delphi winter)**
— the longest chapter is ~7.5× the shortest (the old outline's spread was ~4,800–5,600, ~1.2×).
Deliberate SHORT punch chapters: Ch.4 (1,800), Ch.30 (2,600), Ch.32 (3,200), Ch.36 (1,600).
Deliberate LONG immersive chapters: Ch.11 (12,000), Ch.15 (11,000), Ch.20 (10,800), Ch.6 (10,400),
Ch.13 (10,400), Ch.27 (10,000).

## What changed vs the old 45-chapter outline

**OLD shape:** 45 chapters, all clustered at ~5,200–5,600w (spread ~1.2×, longest ≤2× shortest
by self-imposed cap), split into five labelled ACTS with turning points near the quarter/half
marks and a near-flat chapter-length line — the classic "machine-made uniform" silhouette the
architect rules now flag.

**NEW shape:** **36 chapters**, organized as five **Movements** (dated/place-named, not "Acts"),
built on a **slow-burn-to-eruption** dynamic:
- **Length range 1,600–12,000w** (longest ≈7.5× shortest) instead of a near-flat line. Chapters
  deliberately **swell** through the immersive Greece/Campania movements (Ch.11 Winter 12,000w;
  Ch.15 Building a Life 11,000w; Ch.20 Fenn 10,800w) and **collapse** through the ash (Ch.30
  2,600w; Ch.32 3,200w; Ch.34 3,400w; Ch.36 1,600w) — length itself carries meaning.
- **Short punch chapters** fall exactly where the old outline would have padded to 5,000+: the
  crossing (Ch.4, 1,800w), the first refusal (Ch.30), the door closing (Ch.36, 1,600w).
- **Long immersive chapters** fall on the beats a saga should linger in: the crossing-landing,
  the Delphi winter, the wedding-hinge voyage, the domestic life, the Fenn interview, the
  full eruption.
- **Turning points jittered and back-loaded** (Oracle ~28%, marriage hinge ~39%, midpoint
  reversal ~55%, cruel reveal ~86%) rather than 25/50/75 — an 85%-build / 15%-detonation curve.
- **Count fell from 45 → 36** by *fusing* adjacent same-energy beats into richer chapters
  (Coordinates+Survey; No-Name+Refusal; Leaving-Greece+Marriage; Town-Knows-Her+Sabina;
  Telling+Preparing; door-calls+law-holds; the two closing meditations) while *splintering*
  the catastrophe into short chapters — coarse granularity in the immersion, fine granularity
  in the ash.
- **Σ budget = 273,000w** (≥ 260k target, +5% margin; +53k over the 220k floor) — variance
  redistributes words without undershooting the floor.

All binding canon, characters, theme, motifs, re-read seeds, Fenn document-inserts, and the
drafted Ch.1–2 are preserved; only the container shape changed.
