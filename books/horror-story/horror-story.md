## Premise

John Maxwell runs a bed and breakfast. He's always been able to see ghosts, so when his wife died it was no surprise her ghost stayed with him. His wife had never truly believed him when he told her he could see ghosts, but once she was dead she obviously believed him. There is a wraith or some other entity not only draining energy from the living but the dead as well. So when his dead wife's ghost disappears, he goes hunting. He has put other ghosts to rest before, so he thinks he knows what to do, but this is something he has never encountered before.
