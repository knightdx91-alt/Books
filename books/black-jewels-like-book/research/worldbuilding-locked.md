## Magic system — two axes

**Axis 1 — PLACEMENT = tier (which magics you can access):**
- **Unlit** — no gem, no magic.
- **Left hand (1st)** — the *Inner* magics: telepathy + telekinesis.
- **Left + right (2nd)** — adds the *Outer* craft: shaping, wards, greater reach.
- **Trinity, + forehead (3rd, rare, FORBIDDEN)** — adds a singular **Gift** (one
  per bearer: beast-speech, truth-sight, etc.).
- **Chest (4th, unique to the MC, prophesied)** — the **Keystone**: not raw power
  but a *harmonizing* rank — it can read, kindle, and bind the gems of others.
  This is what makes him a *uniter* (bearers and Domains into concord) and why the
  faction can't simply harvest him like the rest.

**Axis 2 — CELESTIAL GRADE = depth (raw reservoir of each gem):**
Organized as *how much night the stone holds.* Four bands, each lesser/greater
(8 effective grades), weakest → strongest:
**Dawn → Dusk → Star → Void** (Void holds all the night; near-mythic, rarest).
- People read as *placement + grade*: "a left-hand Dusk," "Trinity of Star/Star/Dawn."
- Reading someone's true grade is a *learned skill*; any rank can hide gems from a
  LESSER rank (disguise down or appear Unlit). The MC hides his Trinity + Keystone
  from all lower ranks.

## The cosmology (one chain of cause — everything interlocks)

1. Gems are **Neth's night-light caught in stone.** Neth **(proposed: "Neth, the
   Last Light")** is the goddess of that light.
2. In the founding age Neth bound her ancient opposite — **the First Dark
   (proposed: "Ossuth")**, a primordial titan of hunger that *eats light itself*.
3. To cage it she **poured most of herself into the Binding.** Her **fading ever
   since is the cost of that sacrifice** — and because she *is* the light in the
   gems, her fading is **why deep-grade Stars and Voids are born rarer each
   generation.** (Fading goddess = vanishing Voids = weakening Binding — one fact,
   three faces.)

## The faction (taboo AND hunted, as ONE system)

- **The Vigil (proposed)** — an order founded genuinely to **maintain the Binding**
  by feeding **harvested deep-gems** into it. Many members still believe the
  shield-of-the-world mandate. (This is the gray, real cause.)
- **The Ashen Court (proposed)** — the Vigil's rotten inner cabal. They discovered
  harvested deep-gems also **extend their own lives/power**, so they **hoard the
  surplus to stay immortal and dominant** — skimming fuel the cage needs and thus
  *hastening the very failure they're sworn to prevent.*
- **The Trinity taboo is their supply line:** outlawing the 3rd rank on stated
  grounds of piety ("no mortal should hold such power") means any bearer can be
  **disappeared as a criminal** and harvested. The taboo ENABLES the hunt.
- **On-page antagonists stay HUMAN (the Ashen Court).** Ossuth is the ticking
  cosmic clock beneath the human story — felt through its leaking influence (a
  spreading **un-light**, a *symptom* of the titan, not a rival on-page force),
  mostly off-page.

## The hero

- **17-yr-old orphan, one-rank** (a single left-hand **Dawn** — a nobody). Enlists
  in the **Domash army** (the capital war-Domain's own — he serves inside the very
  power that outlaws his rank). Army recruits dedicate **on enlistment at 17**,
  ahead of the civilian maturity rite (18 M / 16 F). No house to sponsor him at the
  great temple, so he dedicates **alone at a backwater shrine** — which is why the
  disguised goddess finds him solo.
- At the dedication Neth (as a kindly old woman) gifts him the **chest Keystone**
  and jumps him to a **Trinity plus Keystone of shockingly deep grade.** She warns
  of the prophecy and the danger; he resolves to **hide his 3rd and 4th gems.**

## The prophecy & theme

- **Prophecy:** the **Keystone-King** can do what a dying goddess and parasitic
  hoarders cannot — **unite living bearers so their SHARED light renews the
  Binding** (and, should Ossuth break free, lets the Domains *stand* against it
  rather than merely re-cage it).
- **Theme (spine):** **hoarded dying light vs. shared living light.** Toppling the
  Ashen Court is not the whole answer — replacing an unsustainable *cage* (a few
  immortals strip-mining a fading goddess) with a real *stand* (Domains united) is.
