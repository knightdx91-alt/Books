## Premise page

17 yr old male joining the Domash army, he's a one-rank orphan. He chooses to go to a small temple/shrine by himself to do his dedication, not the large temple most go to. A kindly old woman, who is actually a goddess, helps him with the dedication. After, when he realizes he has 4 gems, she tells him some things she has "heard" about a prophecy that mentions a 4th gem. She warns him it would be dangerous to have others find out about his jump in rank as well as the 4th gem, so the guy decides to hide his 3rd and 4th gem. As he is the only one with a 4th gem, he can hide them from all others.

## Worldbuilding / book ideas (got from Black Jewels trilogy by Anne Bishop)

- When born you either have a gem on your left hand or left and right. OR you may be born with no gem, meaning you have no magic.
- It is possible to be born with the "Trinity"? — left hand, right hand, and center of forehead — but it is really rare.
- When you reach the age of maturity, 16 in females and 18 in males, you travel to the High priest or priestess in your Domain to do your dedication ceremony.
- Completing your Dedication ceremony could result in you obtaining a new gem, or in other words a new rank.
- Ranks are as follows: No gem; gem on left hand; gem on left and right; and gem on left, right, and center of forehead.
- In addition, the rank also depends on the gem the person wears, as each gem is given a rank (to be decided later).
- There are different gems for the left hand, different for the right, and different for the forehead.
- Having gems on the left or left and right gives you access to basic magic (telepathy + telekinesis); having one on your forehead grants you an ability (such as talking to animals etc.).
- Areas are separated into Domains.
