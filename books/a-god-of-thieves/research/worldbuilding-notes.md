# Shadow God — Worldbuilding Notes (author's handwritten notes, transcribed)

> Source: author's handwritten notes page, transcribed 2026-07-12. These are the author's
> settled/working canon for the magic system and cosmology. Binding intent — the architect
> builds the bible on top of these, resolving gaps but not contradicting them.

## Genre
Fantasy.

## Magic — how it works
- **Inherent magic is RARE.** Most people have none.
- **Artificers** can make magical items for everyday people — but the items are **VERY expensive**
  (so in practice mostly High Lords/Ladies can afford them; cf. the Singing Siren shop in Draft B).
- The story follows an **orphan who HAS magic but is NOT part of one of the established family
  lines.** He is a **"quirk"** — the **start of a NEW family line.**
- His new bloodline is a **"shadow bloodline"** → think **skills for assassins & thieves.**

## Family lines (bloodlines)
Each family line descends from / is tied to a god, and grants access to that god's domain of magic:
- **Artificers** (the only "known" bloodline publicly; tight rein on their birthing records)
- **Were line**
- **Elemental line**
- **Adaptability line**
- **Spirit line**
- **Necromancer** (necromancer line)
- **(NEW) Shadow line** — Theran's, just beginning.

**Rule:** *everyone in a family line has access to ALL of that line's magic, but some are stronger
in some skills than others.* Also: **people in a family line get a RANDOM ability of the god[s].**
(Reconcile these two during the architect pass — likely: access to the whole domain, but each
person manifests a random signature/strength within it.)

## The gods & the cosmology (the central mythology)
- There is a pantheon of gods, each tied to a domain/bloodline. Gods need **mortal connections**
  (worshippers / the family lines) to sustain their power.
- **Utarra** — an **invading country** wielding **chaos, corruption, and destruction magic**;
  it has its own **Utarran gods**.
- **The God of Shadows was KILLED** ("OG god was killed"). → **Theran is chosen as the new
  God [of Shadows].** This is the title arc: street orphan → new Shadow God.
- **The Artificer God's betrayal (backstory / central conspiracy):**
  - The **Artificer God made a deal with the gods of Utarra**: in exchange for the Artificer God
    **killing the other gods**, the Utarran gods **wouldn't invade** — because the realm would no
    longer be strong enough to be a threat.
  - The Artificer God then **sealed the Domains of the other (murdered) gods**, with help from the
    Utarran gods — which **cut those gods off from their needed mortal connections.** The only
    reason this was possible: the gods **knew how to reach each other's Domains.**
  - Implication: the old gods are dead/sealed, their bloodlines orphaned or fading, and the
    Artificer line now holds outsized power — while Utarra looms. Theran, as the reborn Shadow
    God via a brand-new bloodline, is a crack in the Artificer God's sealed order.

## Main character
- **Theran** (formerly "Azolth" — RENAMED by author 2026-07-12). Orphan street thief, ~10,
  unnatural affinity for shadow/darkness; the quirk who founds the Shadow bloodline and is
  chosen as the new God of Shadows.
