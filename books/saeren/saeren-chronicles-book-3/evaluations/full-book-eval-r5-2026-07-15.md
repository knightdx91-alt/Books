- **Ch.6 local fix (optional, 5 min):** in the single Sela scene, reduce "did not weep" from 4 firings to 2 (keep l.45 opening statement + l.83 closing "carried Sela down"; drop the l.45 internal echo and l.61 restatement). One scene, subtraction only.

It is a same-day, subtraction-only, literary-preserving pass on ~10–12 sentences total. **Do NOT** extend it to the "balance"/"be the wall"/"the count"/"cold working part" vocabulary — that clustering is function or identity, and cutting it would damage the through-book voice.

The r4 thinning did its job: the two clusters the r3 evaluator and beta panel named as saturating — "very long way off" (now ≤2/chapter, back-third only) and the eye-burn restraint-metronome (now ~1/chapter back-third, Ch.19 the one at-ceiling case) — are within acceptable literary bounds on the current text, verified by exact counts. The one residual over-line liability is Pattern #11 ("the way you…"), which r4 never addressed and which shifted the back-third signature-load onto itself (Ch.19 at 1.53/1K). Thinning it in Ch.17–20 is an optional, subtraction-only polish that would lift the average ~0.1; it is not required to ship and not a defect to repair — it is a dose to lower.

**BIAS CHECK:** This evaluation was produced by the same system lineage that wrote the prose. The phrase-density findings here are mechanical (exact grep counts, reproducible) and thus bias-resistant; the 8.5 dimension scores are not, and any score treated as 8.5+ should be validated against an independent human read — particularly Pacing, where the intrasystem reader is the least reliable judge of a quiet book's drag.
