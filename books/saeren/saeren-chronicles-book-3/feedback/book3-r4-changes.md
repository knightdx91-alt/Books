## Edits made

### Fix 3 — thin the motif clusters in Ch.17–19 (the over-saturation the evaluator counted)
- **Ch.17** (climax): three "the very long way off" instances → **two**. Varied the middle one (the
  Bella-thread beat, "keep her edges … for the whole of the very long way off" → "for as long as the
  watch ran") so the phrase no longer rings three times in one section; kept the two climactic
  bookends (the realization + the cost landing), which both pair it with "in her own two hands."
- **Ch.18**: three "very long way off" → **one**. Cut the motif clause from the "holding the door
  true through every birth and every death" sentence; reworded the births passage's "in the fullness
  of the very long way off, end" (it was a near-verbatim cross-chapter twin of a Ch.19 line) to "in
  the years without number ahead of her, to feel every one of them end."
- **Ch.19** (densest — the evaluator's single highest-value target): five "very long way off" →
  **two**; four eye-burn/"looked down until it passed" → **three** (each at a distinct emotional
  peak: the cave-mouth being-held, the Drake goodbye, the closing). Removed the redundant second
  eye-burn beat inside the single cave-mouth scene (it fired twice in adjacent paragraphs); trimmed
  the motif from the early "her hand would stay in the source" line and from the "out of the habit of
  the whole of the very long way off" doubling in the Drake goodbye; kept the two thematic peaks
  (the night-reflection wonder/grief and the final line).

### Fix 2 — differentiate the door-close chapter-END cadence (Ch.2/4/6/8 read episodic)
- Ch.6 and Ch.8 already close distinctively (a human grief-image with Sela's name; a line of dialogue).
  The hard rhyme was **Ch.2 ≈ Ch.4** — both closed on the same "small true thing, the one she always
  knew before she understood it" pre-line plus a "there would be more doors" refrain.
- **Ch.4** re-cadenced: kept Ch.2's "small true thing / more doors" as its signature; Ch.4 now lands
  uniquely — the filed thought finally *arrives* as a **rate** (the gap between opening and closing
  widening; no mark that asks nothing can close a gap that grows) and pulls forward to the coming
  cost ("something she did not have yet, and did not want"). Escalates the sequence instead of
  re-ringing it. (Answers the external r3 review's "ensure each door advances uniquely.")

### Fix 1 — consolidate the Ch.10→11 interiority stack (the book's one real sag)
- The un-mend deliberation lands fully in Ch.10. In **Ch.11** it was re-derived at length (paragraphs
  re-explaining the mechanics — the weave thinned, the cold stopping its lean, the next Sela not
  happening — plus a full "the cold part ran it / I will not do it" pass) *before* the load-bearing
  beat, which is Viridia confessing the temptation aloud to Amber (the new work Ch.10 doesn't do).
  Collapsed ~180 words of re-derivation into a two-sentence confirming reference ("She had known that
  at the pedestal … the same shape the council's mercy had") and go straight to the spoken beat.
  Ch.11's confession-to-Amber, garden decision, and packed-family scenes are untouched.
