## Anti-AI Scan — Literary thresholds (0-3 clean / 4-6 watch / 7+ fingerprint)
**Result: ~4-5 → WATCH band.** Driven almost entirely by two related patterns:

- **#11 Explanatory Extension — STRONG (the primary flag).** The signature "…the way you [do X]" self-completing simile recurs at high density. Ch.1 alone (~4,900 w): "the way you know a lamp has been lit in a far room…"; "the way you do not turn over a stone you have already decided is only a stone"; "the way you fail not to hear a clock in a quiet house"; "the way you remind yourself of a sum" — ~0.8/1K, over the literary 0.5/1K line. It is deliberate, consistent-across-three-books voice (external reviewers praised the atmosphere), so I read it as instrument, not fingerprint — but it is predictable, and it is the pipeline's known tic. This is the single thing to watch.
- **#18 Thematic Echo Chamber — MODERATE.** A recurring closed vocabulary does much of the book's connective work: "the cold working part," "the count / the line runs to my hand," "small true things," "the flat careful surface," "you have to be the wall," "the doorpost / the far end of the bond," "born and gone," "she did not weep / a burning you looked down until it passed." Individually earned; in aggregate, saturating.
- **#16 Philosophical Asides — MINOR, mostly earned** ("fixing was finding someone else to do the holding," Ch.13). Coffee-mug-extractable but load-bearing.
- **#8 Parallelism/anaphora — MINOR, allowlisted/deliberate.**
- #3 rule-of-three: occasional. #14 emotional-control: present but IS the character (intentional).
- Clear: #1, #2, #4 (em-dash gated ≤4), #5, #6, #7, #9, #12, #13, #15, #17 (dialogue is interrupted/naturalistic), #19 (see pacing), #20.

## Per-Chapter Weak Spots (all still ≥ 8.5; these are the thinning targets)
1. **Ch.11 — softest chapter.** The un-mend temptation and "the lines run to my hand" accounting are re-run at length *after* Ch.10 already landed them fully. The Lest-letter END HOOK is the live engine; the twice-told deliberation delays it. **Direction:** collapse the re-deliberation to one confirming beat; let Ch.10 own the reckoning; pull the letter forward. (This is the developmental record's #1 target and I concur.)
2. **Door-chapters 2 / 4 / 6 / 8 — episodic refrain (Pattern #19).** Each is individually strong, but they share a shape (tear appears → she closes it → reflects on the free/easy cost → "there will be more"). Events already escalate (costly mend → Death-mark → symptom-not-cause at Marick's); it's the *closing cadence* that reads same-y. **Direction:** differentiate each door's END by stakes/method/revelation, not by adding plot.
3. **Ch.17-19 — motif over-saturation where events are thin.** "the eyes burned and she looked down until it passed / a burning you looked down until it passed does not count," "the very long way off," "the doorpost / the second warmth" cluster. **Direction:** thin to one load-bearing instance per chapter.
4. **Mid-book thesis restatement.** "Balance, not victory," "you have to be the wall," "the free good thing is how you lose yourself" are stated many times across Ch.4/9/13/17/20. The ideas are excellent; the *repetition* is the drag. Trust the reader to hold them after the second full statement.

## Four/Five-Reader Simulation
- **Devourer:** Will feel the mid-book slow. Ch.8 (Marick) and Ch.16-17 (climax) pull hard; Ch.10-13 is where a fast reader risks drifting. The reflective register is near-constant — variation in *reading speed*, not just in content, is the lever.
- **Critic:** Will underline heavily and admire the cosmology and the refusal-of-power spine. Will also be the one who *notices the "the way you…" construction* and the motif vocabulary and flags them as the tell. Their praise and their one reservation are the same feature at different doses.
- **Hostile:** Weakest attack surface is "nothing much *happens* for stretches — it's a girl feeling the same true thing in prettier words." Defensible as literary intent, but the twice-told deliberations (Ch.11) hand the Hostile reader real ammunition. Fix those and the charge weakens.
- **Casual:** Ch.1 is a strong, confident open (the almond tree, Raizen and the apple, the wrong death END HOOK) — a browser reads on. Sustains interest through vibe and voice more than plot propulsion; the quiet-literary positioning is honest about who this is for.
- **Devoted Reader (active — SFF/series):** This is the reader the book is *built for*. Bookends (almond tree, the socket paying off the Bk2 void-rim seed), Bella-thread payoff (Ch.13: the thread leads not to where her mother *is* but where she *went*), the Fen finale threading to Masters of the Void — all reward the re-reader. High CVI-Legacy signal for this cohort.

## VERDICT
**PASS.** Genesis Floor 8.5, Average ~8.6. The manuscript clears the gate on all seven dimensions and is, on sentence and concept, the strongest-written of the three books. It is delivery-ready as literary YA. The only substantive craft note is not a defect to fix but a dose to lower: the book's own signature (Pattern #11 explanatory extension + Pattern #18 motif saturation + twice-told mid-book deliberation) is over-concentrated in Ch.10-13 and Ch.17-19. Thinning it is polish, not repair.
