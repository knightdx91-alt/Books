# Logline — *A Bond of Scale and Silver*

> Standalone adult romantasy · ~154,000 words

## Logline (one line)

The first natural-born vampire in a thousand years, hidden her whole life, is exposed at a banquet to start a war she never chose — and thrown onto the road with the chief's son who has just shifted into a dragon, the one creature more hunted than she is.

## Hook (2–3 sentences)

Amelia was raised as her mother's cherished secret: a contraband girl with blood magyk that should not exist, kept behind a bolt because the whole world would kill her for being born. When a grief-poisoned schemer unveils her before both courts — forcing the shifter chief into a war he can't win either way — she runs, and the only person who sees a woman instead of a weapon is Korvan, a late-shifter outsider whose first change makes him a clan of one: a dragon out of dead legend. Two hunted things learn each other on the road while the war closes behind them, and the cost of finally being seen comes due at a crown neither of them wanted.
