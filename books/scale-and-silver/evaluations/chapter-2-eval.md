## CROSS-CHAPTER FINGERPRINT (the top finding)
Ch.1 and Ch.2 are both Amelia-POV and **end on the identical beat**: Amelia alone, on/near the bed, resolved, rehearsing her escape plan in the dark, explicitly likened to how her mother taught her to recite the treaty.
- Ch.1 close: "settled in to lie awake and make her plans, very quietly and very well."
- Ch.2 close (¶149): "she began to go over the turnings in the dark behind her eyes, one by one, the way her mother had taught her to go over the treaty, until she knew them well enough to walk them blind."
Two-for-two on the same closing image AND the same "the way mother taught the treaty" simile. This is Pattern #19 territory. Ch.3 is Korvan POV so the streak breaks naturally, but this is the single most valuable thing to vary — if the next Amelia chapter closes the same way, it hardens into a signature the reader will feel.
