## Prose opening (draft)

The Cybernetic Initiative Association, or CIA for short, was in charge of all human-computer
integrations. Their goal since the beginning has been to integrate technology not only into
living things but also non-living. Mark, who was a technologist due to his high affinity for
cybernetic enhancements, was one of the founders of the association.

---

## Structure notes — THREE timelines / three protagonists ("Trio")

**1,000 years ago — MARK.** A technologist, master of many [fields]. Gets rare information
brought to his project — a possible breakthrough in nanotech. Mysterious strangers start staring
at him about town. He learns people have been replaced with **doppelgangers.** (Founder of the
CIA — the Cybernetic Initiative Association.)

**Current time — DARIN MARKSON.** A few hundred years old. His entire life he remembers
technology integrated into all living and non-living objects. Searching through remnants of the
family estate, he reads his grandparents' **nanobot startup company** notes and papers. Darin
finds an old small device — an **injector** — that injects him with an **original nanobot AI**
that gives him **root** and allows him **new freedoms in the real world.**

**500 years ago — THE BOY (on the ship "Hope").** The boy has been wandering the halls of the
spaceship **"Hope,"** deep in space, far from mankind. The kid's **"parents"** (creators)
created him to **restore humanity to the way it was before...**

## What this establishes (for the architect)

- **Premise engine:** nanotech / human-computer integration across a millennium; the CIA
  (Cybernetic Initiative Association) as the world-shaping institution; a hidden doppelganger
  takeover; a lineage (Mark → ... → Darin Markson) tied to the founding nanobot tech; and a
  created "boy" on the ark-ship Hope meant to restore old humanity.
- **Structure:** a braided **three-timeline** novel (1,000 yrs ago / 500 yrs ago / current),
  each with its own protagonist, presumably converging on the doppelganger/nanobot mystery and
  the fate of humanity.
- **Motifs:** "root" (gaining root access to reality via the original nanobot AI); doppelgangers;
  tech in all living AND non-living things; restoration of "true" humanity.
